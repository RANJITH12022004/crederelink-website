The workflow

Five steps, one disciplined process

  1. 01

    Client profiling

    We begin by understanding the business — its operations, financials, credit history, and the precise funding requirement.

  2. 02

    Documentation & solutioning

    The right financing structure is designed and a complete, lender-ready documentation package is assembled.

  3. 03

    Mandate closure

    Scope, terms, and timelines are agreed and formalized before the proposal moves to lenders.

    Lender submission

    The pre-vetted profile is presented to matched lenders, accelerating review cycles and approval timelines.

    Disbursal coordination

    We coordinate approvals, final documentation, and disbursement — with transparent tracking at every milestone.
